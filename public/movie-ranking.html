The Best Movies According to James

My top 3 movies of all-time

Casablanca (1942)

I have loved this film ever since I first saw it as a teenager and it sparked my life long obsession with Humphrey Bogart. With its colourful cast of characters each scene is a stand out classic.

The Big Lebowski (1998)

More a comedy than a crime film with fun characters and zany situations it is no wonder this film has become a cult classic.

The Matrix (1999)

A fun action martial arts sci-fi with the opportunity for discussion about its deeper meaning if you feel like it.
